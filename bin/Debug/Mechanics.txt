HOW TO PLAY

PLAYER vs. PLAYER:
1. The first player clicks a grid to place their chip on the board.
2. After the first player places the chip on the board, the second player clicks a grid on the board.
3. Whoever connects their chip in 4 in a row, whether horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, earns a point.
4. The player who gets two points first wins the game.

PLAYER vs. BOT:
1. The player takes the first turn to click a grid and place their chip on the board.
2. After the player places the chip, the bot takes a turn to click on a grid.
3. Whoever connects their chip in 4 in a row, whether horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, earns a point.
4. The first to get two points wins the game.

PLAYER vs. PLAYER (FREE MODE):
1. The first player chooses to click on any grid to place their chip on the board.
2. After a player places the chip, another player chooses to click on any grid.
3. Whoever connects their chip in 4 in a row, whether horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, earns a point.
4. The player who gets two points first wins the game.

NOTE: Once a player clicks a grid, they are not allowed to click on that grid again.
